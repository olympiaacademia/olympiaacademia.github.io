Olympia Academia is a society with roots dating back to its foundation in 1902 by the brilliant minds of Albert Einstein, Conard Habicht, and Maurice Solovine. Born out of a shared passion for philosophy, physics, and mathematics, this society was established to foster intellectual discussions and exploration.

Over the years, Olympia Academia has evolved, and today, our mission remains rooted in the vision of Albert Einstein himself - the pursuit of a unified theory of everything. We are more than just a science club; we are a vibrant community of passionate students united by a common purpose: to inspire, guide, and empower.

Founded in 2019 at Aligarh Muslim University, we have embarked on a transformative journey to revolutionize the way students experience the worlds of Physics and Mathematics. We recognize the ups and downs that accompany the path of a science student - from the initial burst of enthusiasm to the challenges of navigating the intricate landscape of academia.
